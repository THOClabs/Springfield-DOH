## Synopsis

Bart eats a jagged metal Krusty-O from his cereal and is rushed to the hospital for emergency surgery. While there, Lisa discovers her beloved jazz mentor Bleeding Gums Murphy is also hospitalized—dying. Murphy passes away, leaving Lisa devastated. Bart wins a $100,000 malpractice settlement from Krusty-O's, but it's all spent on Lisa honoring Murphy's memory. Lisa uses the money to buy Murphy's rare album "Sax on the Beach" and convinces a radio station to play it, ensuring his music lives on. The episode is one of the series' most emotional, dealing with death and legacy.

## Scene-by-Scene Breakdown

### Opening Sequence

**Chalkboard Gag:** "I will not simulate flatulence in class" (Bart writes with jagged O's post-surgery)
**Couch Gag:** The living room is an M.C. Escher-style impossible staircase illusion.

---

### ACT ONE

#### Scene 1: Breakfast with Krusty-O's
**Location:** Simpson Kitchen
**Characters:** Bart, Homer, Lisa, Marge

Bart pours a bowl of Krusty-O's cereal for breakfast.

**Bart:** "Mmm, Krusty-O's. The only cereal with the Krusty seal of approval!"

He eats eagerly—and suddenly swallows something sharp.

**Bart:** "AHHH! Something cut me!"

He's swallowed a jagged metal Krusty-O.

**Homer:** "Call 911! Or whatever number is less than 911!"

#### Scene 2: Emergency Room
**Location:** Springfield General Hospital
**Characters:** Bart, Homer, Marge, Lisa, Dr. Hibbert, Dr. Nick

Bart is rushed to the hospital. Dr. Nick nearly operates before Dr. Hibbert intervenes.

**Dr. Nick:** "Hi, everybody! Let's open him up!"

**Dr. Hibbert:** "Step aside. This requires a professional."

Bart undergoes surgery to remove the metal piece.

**Marge:** "Will he be okay?"

**Dr. Hibbert:** "He'll be fine. But he'll need to stay overnight."

#### Scene 3: Lisa Discovers Murphy
**Location:** Springfield General Hospital - Another Wing
**Characters:** Lisa, Bleeding Gums Murphy

While Bart recovers, Lisa wanders the hospital and hears saxophone music.

**Lisa:** "That sound... it's beautiful."

She follows it to a room where Bleeding Gums Murphy lies in bed, playing weakly.

**Lisa:** "Bleeding Gums Murphy! What are you doing here?"

**Murphy:** "Hey, little Lisa. I'm sick, baby. Real sick."

Lisa is heartbroken to see her mentor so ill.

#### Scene 4: Reconnecting
**Location:** Hospital Room
**Characters:** Lisa, Bleeding Gums Murphy

Lisa and Murphy talk about jazz, life, and music.

**Murphy:** "You still playing that saxophone?"

**Lisa:** "Every day. You taught me to love jazz."

**Murphy:** "That makes me happy, little one."

Murphy tells Lisa about his only album, "Sax on the Beach," which nobody bought.

**Murphy:** "I only made one album. It disappeared. Nobody remembers Bleeding Gums Murphy."

**Lisa:** "I remember you."

---

### ACT TWO

#### Scene 5: Murphy's Death
**Location:** Hospital Room
**Characters:** Lisa, Bleeding Gums Murphy, Dr. Hibbert

Lisa visits Murphy again, but this time he's weaker.

**Murphy:** "Lisa... keep playing. Promise me."

**Lisa:** "I promise."

Murphy closes his eyes. The heart monitor flatlines.

**Dr. Hibbert:** "I'm sorry, Lisa. He's gone."

Lisa weeps. Bleeding Gums Murphy is dead.

#### Scene 6: Lisa's Grief
**Location:** Simpson Home / School
**Characters:** Lisa, Family, Students

Lisa is devastated by Murphy's death. She can't focus on anything.

**Marge:** "Lisa, honey, I know you're hurting."

**Lisa:** "He was my friend. The only one who understood me musically."

**Homer:** "There, there. People die all the time. Just like that."

**Marge:** "Homer!"

Lisa isolates herself, unable to process the loss.

#### Scene 7: Bart's Lawsuit
**Location:** Courthouse / Lawyer's Office
**Characters:** Bart, Lionel Hutz, Krusty, Krusty-O's Representative

Bart sues Krusty-O's for the metal in his cereal. Lionel Hutz represents him.

**Hutz:** "My client nearly died from your jagged metal cereal!"

**Krusty:** "Hey, I didn't know there was metal in there! I just endorse stuff!"

The company settles for $100,000 to avoid bad publicity.

**Hutz:** "We won! After my fee, you get... let's see... $500."

**Bart:** "What?!"

**Hutz:** "Okay, okay. $100,000. I can't do math."

Bart receives the full settlement.

#### Scene 8: The Funeral
**Location:** Springfield Cemetery
**Characters:** Lisa, Simpsons, Few Mourners

Murphy's funeral is sparsely attended. Almost nobody came.

**Minister:** "We gather to remember Bleeding Gums Murphy... um... that's all we know about him."

**Lisa:** "This isn't right. He deserves better."

Lisa vows to honor Murphy's memory properly.

---

### ACT THREE

#### Scene 9: Finding the Album
**Location:** Record Stores / Android's Dungeon
**Characters:** Lisa, Comic Book Guy, Store Owners

Lisa searches for Murphy's album, "Sax on the Beach."

**Lisa:** "Do you have any Bleeding Gums Murphy albums?"

**Store Owner:** "Who?"

Nobody has heard of him. Finally, she finds one rare copy—for $500.

**Comic Book Guy:** "This is the only known copy. It is exceedingly rare."

**Lisa:** "I don't have $500..."

#### Scene 10: Bart's Gift
**Location:** Simpson Home
**Characters:** Bart, Lisa

Lisa is dejected. Bart finds her crying.

**Bart:** "What's wrong?"

**Lisa:** "I found Murphy's album, but it costs $500. I wanted to play it on the radio so people would remember him."

Bart thinks about his $100,000 settlement.

**Bart:** "Lisa... take my money."

**Lisa:** "What? Bart, that's your settlement."

**Bart:** "I don't need it. You need it more."

**Lisa:** "But... why?"

**Bart:** "Because you're my sister. And that dead guy meant a lot to you."

Lisa hugs Bart, overwhelmed with gratitude.

#### Scene 11: Buying the Album
**Location:** Record Store
**Characters:** Lisa, Comic Book Guy

Lisa buys the album with Bart's money.

**Lisa:** "I'll take it."

**Comic Book Guy:** "$500."

Lisa hands over the cash and clutches the album.

**Lisa:** "Now the hard part."

---

### ACT FOUR

#### Scene 12: The Radio Station
**Location:** KJAZZ Radio Station
**Characters:** Lisa, Steve Allen, DJ

Lisa goes to the jazz radio station to get Murphy's album played.

**Lisa:** "Please, you have to play this album. Bleeding Gums Murphy deserves to be heard."

**Steve Allen:** "Bleeding Gums Murphy? I knew him! He was a genius!"

**DJ:** "If Steve Allen says play it, we play it."

They put on "Sax on the Beach."

#### Scene 13: Murphy's Music Plays
**Location:** Springfield / Various Locations
**Characters:** All of Springfield

Murphy's saxophone fills the airwaves of Springfield. People across town hear it.

**Homer:** (in car) "Hey, this is pretty good."

**Moe:** (in bar) "Who is this? It's beautiful."

**Jazz Fans:** "That's Bleeding Gums Murphy! He's finally getting played!"

Murphy's music touches the whole town.

#### Scene 14: Murphy in the Clouds
**Location:** Springfield - Sky
**Characters:** Lisa, Ghost of Bleeding Gums Murphy, Mufasa, Darth Vader, James Earl Jones

Lisa looks at the sky. Clouds form the image of Bleeding Gums Murphy.

**Murphy's Ghost:** "Lisa, you've done a wonderful thing."

**Lisa:** "Murphy? Is that you?"

**Murphy's Ghost:** "You made sure my music will live on. Thank you."

The clouds shift. Other figures appear (parody of The Lion King).

**Mufasa:** "You must avenge my death, Kimba—I mean, Simba."

**Darth Vader:** "Luke, I am your father."

**James Earl Jones:** "This is CNN."

Murphy laughs and fades away.

**Murphy's Ghost:** "Keep playing, Lisa."

Lisa smiles through her tears.

#### Scene 15: Resolution
**Location:** Simpson Home
**Characters:** Simpson Family

Lisa is at peace with Murphy's death.

**Marge:** "How are you feeling, Lisa?"

**Lisa:** "Better. I made sure people will remember him."

**Bart:** "That's $99,500 well spent."

**Homer:** "Wait, you spent all of Bart's money?"

**Bart:** "It's fine, Homer. Lisa needed it."

**Homer:** "But... but... the money..."

**Lisa:** "Some things are more important than money, Dad."

**Homer:** "...Are they, though?"

**Marge:** "HOMER."

The family laughs. Lisa picks up her saxophone and plays, Murphy's spirit living on in her music.
