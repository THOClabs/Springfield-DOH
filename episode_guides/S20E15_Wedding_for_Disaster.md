## Synopsis

When it's revealed that Reverend Lovejoy wasn't properly licensed during a three-month period, Homer and Marge's remarriage ceremony is invalidated. Homer proposes again, but on their wedding day, he mysteriously disappears, leaving only a keychain marked "SB." While Bart and Lisa suspect Sideshow Bob, Homer is actually being held captive by Patty and Selma in a "Saw"-style torture scenario. The twins plan to keep Homer imprisoned until Marge forgets him, but his heartfelt wedding vows ultimately move them to tears.

## Scene-by-Scene Breakdown

### Opening Sequence
**Chalkboard Gag:** "My piggy bank is not entitled to TARP funds"
**Couch Gag:** The Simpson family portrayed as courses in a fancy dinner, consumed by Comic Book Guy

---

### ACT ONE

#### Scene 1: The Invalid Marriage
**Location:** First Church of Springfield
**Characters:** Reverend Lovejoy, congregation, Homer, Marge

A shocking revelation: Reverend Lovejoy was not properly licensed as a minister during a three-month period. All official ceremonies he performed during that time are invalidated - including Homer and Marge's remarriage ceremony.

#### Scene 2: The Discovery
**Location:** Simpson Home / Church
**Characters:** Homer, Marge, family

Homer and Marge learn that they are technically not married. Their previous remarriage (which occurred during the unlicensed period) is not legally binding.

#### Scene 3: Homer Proposes Again
**Location:** 742 Evergreen Terrace
**Characters:** Homer, Marge

Homer takes the opportunity to propose to Marge again, making it romantic. Marge accepts, and they plan another wedding ceremony.

#### Scene 4: Wedding Preparations
**Location:** Various
**Characters:** Simpson family, wedding guests

The family prepares for the new wedding ceremony. Guests are invited, and everything seems to be falling into place.

---

### ACT TWO

#### Scene 5: Homer Disappears
**Location:** Wedding venue
**Characters:** Marge, Bart, Lisa, wedding guests

On their wedding day, Homer mysteriously vanishes. He doesn't show up to the ceremony. The only clue left behind is a keychain marked "SB."

#### Scene 6: Suspicion Falls on Sideshow Bob
**Location:** Various
**Characters:** Bart, Lisa, Chief Wiggum, police

Bart and Lisa immediately suspect Sideshow Bob based on the "SB" initials. They begin investigating and following clues that seem to point to Bob.

#### Scene 7: Homer's Captivity
**Location:** Dark room / Dungeon setting
**Characters:** Homer, mysterious voice (Patty/Selma with modulator)

Homer finds himself chained to a pipe in a dark room. A mysterious voice (using a voice modulator) taunts him. The scenario parodies the "Saw" horror film franchise.

#### Scene 8: The Lollipop Torture
**Location:** Captivity location
**Characters:** Homer, mysterious voice

The mysterious voice tells Homer that the key to unlock his chains is inside a lollipop. He frantically eats it, only to discover the lollipop is made of fiery hot sauce. He is tormented while Bart and Lisa race to save him.

#### Scene 9: Following the Clues
**Location:** Springfield - Various
**Characters:** Bart, Lisa

Bart and Lisa follow a cryptic trail of clues throughout Springfield. Each clue seems to lead them closer to finding Homer and confirming Sideshow Bob's involvement.

---

### ACT THREE

#### Scene 10: Confronting Sideshow Bob
**Location:** Sideshow Bob's location
**Characters:** Bart, Lisa, Sideshow Bob (Kelsey Grammer)

Bart and Lisa confront Sideshow Bob, believing he kidnapped Homer. However, Bob has an alibi and reveals he's not involved. The "SB" doesn't stand for Sideshow Bob.

#### Scene 11: The Real Culprits
**Location:** Captivity location
**Characters:** Homer, Patty, Selma

The truth is revealed: "SB" stands for Selma Bouvier. Patty and Selma are behind Homer's kidnapping. They orchestrated the entire plot to keep Homer imprisoned until Marge forgets about him.

#### Scene 12: Homer's Wedding Vows
**Location:** Captivity location
**Characters:** Homer, Patty, Selma

Despite his captivity, Homer delivers his heartfelt wedding vows. His sincere and emotional words about loving Marge move Patty and Selma to tears. They are genuinely touched by his devotion.

#### Scene 13: Homer Released
**Location:** Captivity location / Simpson Home
**Characters:** Homer, Patty, Selma, Marge

Moved by Homer's vows, Patty and Selma release him. They realize, perhaps for the first time, how much Homer truly loves their sister.

#### Scene 14: Bart and Lisa's Blackmail
**Location:** Various
**Characters:** Bart, Lisa, Patty, Selma

Bart and Lisa discover what their aunts did. They subsequently blackmail Patty and Selma into funding the remarriage ceremony by threatening to expose their involvement in Homer's kidnapping.

#### Scene 15: The Remarriage
**Location:** Wedding venue
**Characters:** Homer, Marge, family, guests

Homer and Marge finally get remarried in a proper ceremony, funded by Patty and Selma. Their marriage is once again legal and binding.
