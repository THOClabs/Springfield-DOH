## Synopsis

A new student named Allison Taylor arrives at Springfield Elementary, and Lisa is shocked to discover that Allison is everything she is—but better. Allison is smarter, more talented at saxophone, and even a grade younger. For the first time, Lisa faces a true rival, and jealousy consumes her. When the school diorama competition approaches, Lisa is tempted to sabotage Allison's superior entry. Meanwhile, Homer stumbles upon an overturned sugar truck and becomes obsessed with hoarding and selling the sugar, dreaming of riches until bees steal his stash.

## Scene-by-Scene Breakdown

### Opening Sequence

**Chalkboard Gag:** "No one is interested in my underpants"
**Couch Gag:** The family performs a kickline with Rockettes.

### ACT ONE

#### Scene 1: The New Student
**Location:** Springfield Elementary - Classroom
**Characters:** Lisa, Miss Hoover, Allison Taylor, Students

Miss Hoover introduces a new student: Allison Taylor.

**Miss Hoover:** "Class, we have a new student. This is Allison Taylor."

Lisa is friendly at first, happy to potentially have an intellectual equal.

**Lisa:** "Hi, I'm Lisa Simpson. Welcome to Springfield Elementary."

**Allison:** "Thanks! I've heard you're the smartest kid in class."

**Lisa:** "Well, I do try..."

#### Scene 2: Discovering Allison's Talents
**Location:** School - Various
**Characters:** Lisa, Allison

Lisa quickly discovers Allison is exceptional at everything:
- Answers questions faster than Lisa
- Gets perfect scores
- Is a grade younger (skipped ahead)

**Lisa:** "What grade were you in before?"

**Allison:** "First. I skipped second."

**Lisa:** (internally panicking) "She's younger AND smarter?!"

#### Scene 3: The Saxophone Showdown
**Location:** School Band Room
**Characters:** Lisa, Allison

The biggest blow: Allison plays saxophone—and she's better than Lisa.

**Allison:** (plays a beautiful jazz solo)

**Lisa:** (plays her best, but it's not as good)

**Bandleader:** "Allison, that was magnificent! Lisa, you were... also there."

Lisa is devastated.

#### Scene 4: Homer Finds the Sugar
**Location:** Springfield Streets
**Characters:** Homer

Homer is driving when he sees an overturned sugar truck. Sugar is spilling everywhere.

**Homer:** "Ooh! Free sugar!"

He begins scooping sugar into the car, envisioning riches.

**Homer:** "I'm gonna be rich! Sugar! White gold!"

---

### ACT TWO

#### Scene 5: Lisa's Jealousy Grows
**Location:** Simpson Home / School
**Characters:** Lisa, Marge, Bart

Lisa becomes consumed by jealousy. She can't stop thinking about Allison.

**Lisa:** "She's ruining everything! I used to be special!"

**Marge:** "Lisa, there's room for two smart girls."

**Lisa:** "No there isn't! There's only one 'smartest kid' and now it's her!"

**Bart:** "Sounds like you need to take her down."

**Lisa:** "Bart! I would never... would I?"

#### Scene 6: Homer's Sugar Hoard
**Location:** Simpson Backyard
**Characters:** Homer, Marge

Homer has created a massive pile of sugar in the backyard. He guards it obsessively.

**Marge:** "Homer, you're hoarding sugar in the backyard?"

**Homer:** "It's my ticket to fortune, Marge! Do you know how much sugar is worth?"

**Marge:** "About... thirty-five cents a pound?"

**Homer:** "Exactly! And I've got at least forty pounds!"

**Marge:** "That's fourteen dollars, Homer."

**Homer:** "Fourteen dollars?! Woo-hoo!"

Homer guards his sugar with a shotgun.

#### Scene 7: The Diorama Competition Announced
**Location:** Springfield Elementary
**Characters:** Principal Skinner, Students, Lisa, Allison

Principal Skinner announces the annual diorama competition.

**Skinner:** "This year's theme is 'Great Moments in American History.'"

Lisa sees this as her chance to beat Allison.

**Lisa:** "This is it. I'll make the best diorama ever!"

**Allison:** "Me too! May the best diorama win."

**Lisa:** (forcing a smile) "Yes. May the best diorama win."

#### Scene 8: Visiting Allison's Home
**Location:** Taylor Residence
**Characters:** Lisa, Allison, Professor Taylor

Lisa visits Allison's home and is further intimidated. The Taylors are cultured intellectuals.

**Professor Taylor:** "Ah, Lisa! Allison speaks highly of you."

**Allison:** "Want to see my diorama? It's 'The Tell-Tale Heart' by Edgar Allan Poe!"

Allison's diorama is incredible—a working replica with a beating heart mechanism.

**Lisa:** (devastated) "That's... amazing."

**Allison:** "Thanks! What's yours?"

**Lisa:** "Um... still working on it."

---

### ACT THREE

#### Scene 9: The Sabotage Plan
**Location:** Simpson Home
**Characters:** Lisa, Bart

Desperate, Lisa considers sabotage. Bart offers to help.

**Bart:** "Here's what you do. Switch her diorama with a crummy one."

**Lisa:** "That's horrible! I could never... tell me more."

**Bart:** "We swap her 'Tell-Tale Heart' for a box with a cow heart in it. She'll look crazy."

**Lisa:** "Bart, that's evil... but also my only chance."

Lisa wrestles with her conscience but agrees to the plan.

#### Scene 10: Homer vs. The Bees
**Location:** Simpson Backyard
**Characters:** Homer, Bees

Homer's sugar pile attracts bees. A lot of bees.

**Homer:** "Shoo! Get away from my sugar!"

The bees swarm. Homer fights them off with increasingly futile efforts.

**Homer:** "You can't have it! It's mine!"

Eventually, rain comes and dissolves the sugar, and the bees take what's left.

**Homer:** "Nooo! My sugar! It's gone! Gone forever!"

**Marge:** "Homer, it was always going to end this way."

**Homer:** "I know, Marge. I know."

#### Scene 11: The Switch
**Location:** School - Night
**Characters:** Lisa, Bart

Lisa and Bart sneak into school and switch Allison's diorama with a cow heart in a box.

**Lisa:** "I can't believe I'm doing this."

**Bart:** "Just do it fast before your conscience kicks in."

They make the switch.

#### Scene 12: Lisa's Conscience
**Location:** School Hallway
**Characters:** Lisa

Lisa immediately regrets it. She can't sleep, can't eat.

**Lisa:** (internal monologue) "What have I done? I'm no better than a common cheater!"

She sees Allison's genuine kindness and feels terrible.

---

### ACT FOUR

#### Scene 13: The Diorama Judging
**Location:** School Gymnasium
**Characters:** Lisa, Allison, Skinner, Judges, Students, Ralph

The diorama competition begins. Lisa watches nervously as Allison presents... the cow heart.

**Allison:** "Um... this is 'The Tell-Tale Heart'... it's a real heart... from a cow."

**Skinner:** "That's... disturbing, Allison."

Lisa feels sick. Allison is humiliated.

#### Scene 14: Ralph Wins
**Location:** School Gymnasium
**Characters:** Ralph, Skinner, Lisa, Allison

In a twist, Ralph Wiggum wins the competition with his simple diorama.

**Skinner:** "And the winner is... Ralph Wiggum for 'Star Wars'!"

**Ralph:** "I'm a Star Wars!"

His diorama is just action figures in a shoebox.

**Lisa:** "What?! That's not even American history!"

**Skinner:** "It's compelling, Lisa. Compelling."

Neither Lisa nor Allison wins, making Lisa's sabotage pointless.

#### Scene 15: Lisa Confesses
**Location:** School / Taylor Home
**Characters:** Lisa, Allison

Lisa, guilt-ridden, confesses to Allison.

**Lisa:** "Allison, I have to tell you something. I switched your diorama. I was so jealous of you that I cheated."

**Allison:** "I know."

**Lisa:** "You knew?"

**Allison:** "I figured it out. I was hurt, but I understand. Being the smart kid is hard."

**Lisa:** "You're not mad?"

**Allison:** "I'm a little mad. But I'd rather have a friend than an enemy."

They shake hands, beginning a real friendship.

#### Scene 16: Resolution
**Location:** Simpson Home / School
**Characters:** Lisa, Allison, Homer

Lisa and Allison become genuine friends, respecting each other's talents.

**Lisa:** "I learned something. There's room for more than one smart girl."

**Allison:** "And competition makes us both better."

Meanwhile, Homer mourns his sugar.

**Homer:** "I'll never have another pile of sugar like that."

**Marge:** "You'll get over it, Homer."

**Homer:** (sadly) "I know. But it hurts."
