## Synopsis

When Homer's poker buddies call him "a little slow," he becomes determined to prove his intelligence. Marge suggests adult education, but Homer decides to teach instead - choosing "Secrets of a Successful Marriage" as his only qualified subject. To keep his students engaged, Homer starts sharing embarrassing personal secrets about Marge. When she discovers the betrayal, she kicks him out of the house. Living in Bart's treehouse, Homer must find a way to win Marge back while fending off Moe's romantic advances toward his wife.

### ACT ONE

#### Scene 1: The Poker Game
**Location:** Lenny's House
**Characters:** Homer, Moe, Lenny, Carl, Barney

Homer's bar buddies (Barney, Moe) meet his work buddies (Lenny, Carl) for poker night.

Homer folds his hand. Moe is overjoyed at winning, then grows irritated.

Homer didn't realize he had a straight flush - an excellent hand.

Carl tells Moe not to yell at Homer "just 'cause he's a little slow."

Homer is hurt by being called slow.

#### Scene 2: Homer's Crisis
**Location:** Simpson Home
**Characters:** Homer, Marge

Homer tells Marge about being called slow. He's dejected and worried.

Marge suggests he take an adult education course to sharpen his intellect.

Homer considers this idea.

#### Scene 3: Adult Education Annex
**Location:** Springfield Adult Education Annex
**Characters:** Homer, administrator, various teachers

Homer discovers his friends are all teaching classes:
- **Patty & Selma:** "Turn A Man Into Putty In Your Hands"
- **Moe:** "Funk Dancing For Self-Defense"
- **Lenny:** "How To Chew Tobacco"
- **Hans Moleman:** "How To Eat An Orange"

Homer approaches the administrator for a teaching position.

He is hired to teach "Secrets of a Successful Marriage" - the only subject he's qualified for.

#### Scene 4: Moe's Class Preview
**Location:** Adult Education Annex
**Characters:** Moe, students

Moe demonstrates his "Funk Dancing For Self-Defense" technique:

"Say some gangsta is dissin' ya fly girl. Just give 'em one of these!"

He performs aggressive breakdancing that ends with gunfire.

---

### ACT TWO

#### Scene 5: Homer's First Class
**Location:** Adult Education Annex - Classroom
**Characters:** Homer, students

Homer begins teaching his marriage class.

He struggles to engage his students with generic marriage advice.

His material is boring. Students look uninterested.

#### Scene 6: Homer Improvises
**Location:** Classroom
**Characters:** Homer, students

To keep students engaged, Homer starts sharing personal anecdotes about Marge.

Embarrassing secrets about their marriage and personal life.

Students are suddenly riveted. Class becomes popular.

#### Scene 7: Marge Discovers
**Location:** Various
**Characters:** Marge, townspeople, Homer

Marge hears people mentioning her personal secrets around town.

She realizes Homer has been sharing their private life in class.

Marge confronts Homer. He promises to stop.

#### Scene 8: Homer Breaks His Promise
**Location:** Classroom
**Characters:** Homer, students

Homer tries teaching without personal secrets.

Students are bored again. He can't help himself.

Homer invites students to his house to observe the family at dinner.

---

### ACT THREE

#### Scene 9: The Dinner Observation
**Location:** Simpson Home - Dining Room
**Characters:** Homer, Marge, family, students including Moe

Homer's students observe the Simpson family having dinner.

Moe mentions another personal secret about Marge.

Marge is furious. She realizes Homer never stopped.

She chases the students away and evicts Homer from the house.

#### Scene 10: Homer in the Treehouse
**Location:** Bart's Treehouse
**Characters:** Homer, Bart, Lisa

With nowhere to go, Homer lives in Bart's treehouse.

He deteriorates rapidly without Marge's support.

Bart and Lisa worry their parents will get divorced.

#### Scene 11: Moe Makes His Move
**Location:** Simpson Home
**Characters:** Moe, Marge

Moe sees his opportunity. He brings Marge flowers.

He attempts to romance her while Homer is gone.

Marge is uncomfortable but doesn't outright reject him.

#### Scene 12: Homer's Failed Attempt
**Location:** Simpson Home / Treehouse
**Characters:** Homer, Marge, Lisa

Homer returns with flowers for Marge.

He's devastated to learn Moe already gave her flowers.

Homer retreats to the treehouse.

#### Scene 13: Lisa's Counsel
**Location:** Bart's Treehouse
**Characters:** Homer, Lisa

Homer pleads with Lisa to help him trick Marge into loving him again.

Lisa chastises him: "That's immoral!"

Homer offers to bribe her with $40. She refuses, saying love can't be bought.

He counters with $30. Lisa is unimpressed.

#### Scene 14: Homer's Revelation
**Location:** Treehouse / Simpson Home
**Characters:** Homer, Marge, Moe

Homer realizes he can only offer Marge one thing: complete and utter dependency.

He explains to Marge that he cannot survive without her.

Rather than a weakness, his need for her is his argument - he makes her feel needed.

Marge is hesitant but ultimately touched.

The couple reconciles.

Moe flees in fear when Homer returns.
