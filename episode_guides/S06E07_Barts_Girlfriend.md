## Synopsis

Bart becomes infatuated with Jessica Lovejoy, Reverend Lovejoy's seemingly perfect daughter. After initially rejecting him, Jessica becomes interested in Bart when she sees his rebellious side. They bond over mischief, but Bart discovers Jessica is far more devious than he ever was. When Jessica steals money from the church collection plate and frames Bart, he becomes the town pariah. Only Lisa believes in his innocence and works to expose Jessica's true nature to the congregation.

### ACT ONE

#### Scene 1: Church Service
**Location:** First Church of Springfield
**Characters:** Simpson family, Lovejoy family, congregation

The Simpson family attends church.

Bart notices Jessica Lovejoy, the reverend's daughter. She appears angelic and perfect.

Bart is instantly smitten.

#### Scene 2: Bart's Infatuation
**Location:** Various Springfield locations
**Characters:** Bart, Milhouse

Bart becomes obsessed with Jessica.

He tries various methods to get her attention.

Jessica initially ignores and rejects him.

#### Scene 3: Expelled from Lovejoy Home
**Location:** Lovejoy House
**Characters:** Bart, Jessica, Reverend Lovejoy, Helen

Bart visits the Lovejoy home trying to see Jessica.

He is accused of swearing (though he didn't).

Reverend Lovejoy expels Bart from the house.

#### Scene 4: Jessica Takes Interest
**Location:** Springfield streets
**Characters:** Bart, Jessica

Ironically, being thrown out catches Jessica's attention.

She sees Bart's rebellious reputation and becomes interested.

Jessica approaches Bart. Their relationship begins.

---

### ACT TWO

#### Scene 5: The Strutting Scene
**Location:** Springfield streets
**Characters:** Bart

Bart's iconic moment: "There's only one thing to do at a time like this: strut!"

He walks confidently through Springfield, thrilled about his new girlfriend.

#### Scene 6: Bonding Through Mischief
**Location:** Various Springfield locations
**Characters:** Bart, Jessica

Bart and Jessica engage in petty vandalism and troublemaking together.

Bart is thrilled to have a partner in crime.

They escalate their pranks around the city.

#### Scene 7: Jessica Goes Too Far
**Location:** Various
**Characters:** Bart, Jessica

Bart realizes Jessica's mischief is more extreme than his own.

She's not just a troublemaker - she's genuinely malicious.

Bart grows uncomfortable but is too smitten to break things off.

#### Scene 8: The Milk Dud Realization
**Location:** Simpson Home
**Characters:** Bart, Lisa

Lisa tries to warn Bart about Jessica.

Lisa describes her: "She's like a Milk Dud; sweet on the outside, poison on the inside."

Jessica has an IQ of 108 - smart enough to be dangerous.

Bart dismisses Lisa's concerns.

---

### ACT THREE

#### Scene 9: Church Collection Theft
**Location:** First Church of Springfield
**Characters:** Bart, Jessica, congregation

During church service, Bart sits next to Jessica.

When the collection plate passes, Jessica steals all the money.

She places the empty plate on Bart's lap.

#### Scene 10: Bart Accused
**Location:** First Church of Springfield
**Characters:** Helen Lovejoy, congregation, Bart

Helen Lovejoy notices the empty collection plate.

She calls attention to it. All eyes turn to Bart holding the plate.

The congregation immediately assumes Bart stole the money.

Bart is shunned and blamed. No one believes his denials.

#### Scene 11: Lisa Investigates
**Location:** Simpson Home / Various
**Characters:** Lisa, Bart

Lisa is the only one who believes Bart is innocent.

She confronts Jessica, trying to get her to confess.

Jessica refuses. She enjoys watching Bart suffer.

#### Scene 12: Lisa's Exposure
**Location:** First Church of Springfield
**Characters:** Lisa, Jessica, congregation, Reverend Lovejoy

Lisa addresses the congregation.

She accuses Jessica of being the true thief.

Lisa suggests searching Jessica's room for the stolen money.

#### Scene 13: The Search
**Location:** Lovejoy House - Jessica's Room
**Characters:** Congregation, Jessica, Reverend Lovejoy

The townspeople search Jessica's room.

They find the stolen money hidden under her bed.

Jessica is caught red-handed.

#### Scene 14: Jessica's Confession
**Location:** Lovejoy House
**Characters:** Jessica, Reverend Lovejoy

Reverend Lovejoy expresses disbelief at his daughter's actions.

Jessica reminds him of her past misbehavior - behavior he willfully ignored.

She claims she acted out to get his attention.

#### Scene 15: Resolution
**Location:** First Church of Springfield / Church Steps
**Characters:** Bart, Jessica, congregation, new boyfriend

Bart receives an apology from the congregation.

Jessica is punished: she must scrub the church steps.

But Jessica traps Bart into doing her punishment for her.

She disappears with a new boyfriend, leaving Bart scrubbing.

- **Character design:** Matt Groening found Jessica hard to draw attractively in his style
