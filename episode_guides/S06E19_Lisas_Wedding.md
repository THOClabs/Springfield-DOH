## Synopsis

At a Renaissance fair, a fortune teller shows Lisa a vision of her future. In the year 2010, 23-year-old Lisa is a university student who meets and falls in love with Hugh Parkfield, a charming British intellectual. They become engaged, but when Hugh meets Lisa's family, he's horrified by their behavior. On the wedding day, Hugh reveals he wants Lisa to abandon her family permanently after they're married. Lisa refuses, realizing that despite her complaints, she loves her family unconditionally. She calls off the wedding, choosing the Simpsons over a sophisticated life in England.

### ACT ONE (Present Day - 1995)

#### Scene 1: Renaissance Fair
**Location:** Renaissance Fair
**Characters:** Simpson family

The Simpson family attends a Renaissance fair.

They enjoy medieval entertainment, jousting, and fair food.

Lisa wanders off on her own.

#### Scene 2: The Fortune Teller
**Location:** Fortune Teller's Tent
**Characters:** Lisa, Fortune Teller

Lisa discovers a fortune-telling booth.

The mysterious fortune teller offers to show Lisa her true love.

Lisa is skeptical but intrigued.

#### Scene 3: The Vision Begins
**Location:** Fortune Teller's Tent
**Characters:** Lisa, Fortune Teller

The fortune teller begins her prediction.

The vision transports us to the year 2010 - 15 years in the future.

---

### ACT TWO (Year 2010)

#### Scene 4: Eastern University
**Location:** University Library - England
**Characters:** Lisa (23), Hugh Parkfield

Lisa is now 23, a student at an Eastern university.

She reaches for a book at the same time as Hugh Parkfield, a British student.

They initially clash over who gets the book.

#### Scene 5: Falling in Love
**Location:** Various university locations
**Characters:** Lisa, Hugh

Despite their rocky start, Lisa and Hugh begin spending time together.

They bond over intellectualism, culture, and shared interests.

Hugh is everything Lisa dreamed of - sophisticated, intelligent, worldly.

They fall deeply in love.

#### Scene 6: The Proposal
**Location:** University / Hugh's residence
**Characters:** Lisa, Hugh

Hugh proposes marriage to Lisa.

She joyfully accepts.

They plan to wed in Springfield so Lisa's family can attend.

#### Scene 7: Springfield in 2010
**Location:** Springfield - 2010
**Characters:** Various

The future Springfield is revealed:
- Flying cars and holographic technology
- Smartwatches and video calling
- "Big Ben's" fast food chain
- The Rolling Stones "Steel Wheelchair Tour 2010" poster

Technology has advanced, but Springfield remains essentially Springfield.

#### Scene 8: The Simpson Family - 2010
**Location:** 742 Evergreen Terrace - 2010
**Characters:** Homer, Marge, Bart, Maggie

The future Simpsons:
- **Homer:** Still works at Sector 7G; hasn't changed much
- **Marge:** Still a housewife; same blue hair
- **Bart:** 25, demolition expert, twice divorced, frequents strip clubs
- **Maggie:** Punk teenager who "never shuts up" (but never speaks on screen)

#### Scene 9: Hugh Meets the Family
**Location:** Simpson Home - 2010
**Characters:** Hugh, all Simpsons

Hugh arrives in Springfield to meet Lisa's family.

The Simpsons are their usual chaotic, embarrassing selves.

Hugh is horrified by Homer's behavior, Bart's crudeness, and the general chaos.

He tries to hide his dismay from Lisa.

#### Scene 10: The Pig Cufflinks
**Location:** Simpson Home
**Characters:** Homer, Hugh

Homer presents Hugh with a "family tradition" - pig-shaped cufflinks.

He wants Hugh to wear them at the wedding.

Hugh is appalled but agrees to please Lisa.

---

### ACT THREE (The Wedding Day - 2010)

#### Scene 11: Wedding Preparations
**Location:** Church / Various - 2010
**Characters:** Lisa, Marge, family

Lisa prepares for her wedding day.

Marge helps her daughter get ready.

The whole town prepares for the ceremony.

#### Scene 12: Hugh's Ultimatum
**Location:** Private room - 2010
**Characters:** Lisa, Hugh

Before the ceremony, Hugh speaks privately with Lisa.

He reveals his true feelings: after the wedding, they will return to England permanently.

Hugh insists they never see Lisa's family again - he's too embarrassed by them.

He'll wear the pig cufflinks for the ceremony, then never again.

#### Scene 13: Lisa's Decision
**Location:** Private room - 2010
**Characters:** Lisa, Hugh

Lisa is outraged by Hugh's ultimatum.

She realizes that despite her constant complaints about her family, she loves them.

She cannot marry someone who doesn't understand this fundamental truth about her.

Lisa calls off the wedding.

#### Scene 14: Hugh Departs
**Location:** Church / Airport - 2010
**Characters:** Hugh, Lisa

Hugh leaves Springfield, returning to England alone.

Lisa watches him go, sad but certain of her choice.

She chose her family over sophisticated romance.

#### Scene 15: Family Reunion
**Location:** Simpson Home - 2010
**Characters:** Lisa, Homer, family

Lisa returns to her family.

Homer finds her alone and comforts her.

He doesn't fully understand what happened but offers unconditional support.

Lisa appreciates her family more than ever.

---

### CLOSING (Present Day - 1995)

#### Scene 16: Back at the Fair
**Location:** Fortune Teller's Tent
**Characters:** Lisa, Fortune Teller

The vision ends. Lisa is back in 1995.

She asks the fortune teller if she ever finds true love.

The fortune teller hints that she will - but it's a different person.

Lisa rejoins her family, seeing them with new appreciation.
