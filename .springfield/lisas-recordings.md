## Meta-Analysis

### Patterns Across All Entries

**Pattern 1: The Permission Effect**
Users consistently valued having a "character" ask hard questions. The cartoon provides cover for honesty.

**Pattern 2: The Individual-First Truth**
Multiple users indicated private reflection should precede team sharing.

**Pattern 3: The Cross-Domain Surprise**
Non-technical users found as much value as technical users - sometimes more.

**Pattern 4: The Skeptic-to-Advocate Pipeline**
Initial skepticism often correlated with eventual enthusiasm.

**Pattern 5: The Seed-Planting Value**
Not all impact is immediate. Some users carry questions with them.
